1.2 - 2012-04-26
----------------

- Fix includes to work correctly with dexterity and
  to include simplejson.
  [vangheem]

- Change ping intervals to something more sane so it doesn't
  take so long for your workers to come back online after restart.

----------------

  [stefan]
